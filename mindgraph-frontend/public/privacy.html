Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Karthik Madheswaran ("we," "us," or "our"), the sole operator of rawtxt, collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data when you use the Service at rawtxt.in. We take the privacy of your journal entries seriously. Please read this policy carefully.

1. What We Collect

We collect the following categories of data when you use rawtxt:

  • Account information: your email address and a hashed password, used for authentication via Supabase Auth.
  • Journal entries: the text you submit through the journaling interface. This is the primary content of the Service.
  • Derived data: entities (people, projects, tools), deadlines, behavioral patterns, semantic embeddings, and other structured outputs extracted from your entries by our AI pipeline.
  • Usage data: anonymized product analytics events (such as which features you use and how often), error reports, and session data. These are collected in aggregated, non-identifiable form where possible.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and device type, collected automatically as part of normal web service operation.

We do not collect payment card details directly. Payments are processed by our payment provider, who handles card data under their own PCI-compliant systems.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve the Service for you. Specifically:

  • Journal entries are processed by our AI pipeline to extract entities, deadlines, projects, and patterns, which are then shown on your personal dashboard.
  • Semantic embeddings of your entries are stored to power the Ask Journal feature, which lets you query your own journal history using natural language.
  • Usage analytics help us understand which features are working well and which need improvement.
  • Error reports help us identify and fix bugs.

Your journal entries are never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for any commercial purpose. They are never used to train third-party AI models. They are never used for advertising. They are only ever processed to power your own product experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your journal entries and all derived data are stored in a Supabase Postgres database with encryption at rest. Each user's data is isolated by a row-level security policy that ensures you can only access your own records.

Access to production infrastructure is restricted to the operator. We use industry-standard practices for secrets management, access control, and deployment security. However, no system is completely immune to security incidents. We will notify affected users promptly in the event of a confirmed data breach.

4. Sub-processors

We use the following third-party services to operate rawtxt. Each has access only to the data necessary for their specific function:

Service Purpose Location
Supabase Database storage, authentication, row-level access control United States
Railway Frontend and backend infrastructure hosting United States
Google (Gemini API) AI text processing for entity extraction, summarization, and generation United States
Cohere Reranking of hybrid search results in the Ask Journal pipeline United States / Canada
Upstash Redis cache for rate limiting United States
Sentry Error monitoring and crash reporting United States
PostHog Product analytics (event tracking, session recording with masking) United States
Langfuse AI pipeline observability (latency, token usage, trace logging) European Union

We select sub-processors carefully and review their privacy and security practices. When sending data to these services, we send only what is required for the specific function. We do not send your full journal entries to analytics or monitoring services.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete an entry within the product, it is soft-deleted immediately and permanently purged from our systems within 30 days.

To request deletion of your entire account and all associated data, contact us at karthik@rawtxt.in with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." We will confirm receipt within 3 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days of your request. Backups containing your data will be purged on the next backup rotation cycle after the deletion window.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection law, including the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you, and the right to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at karthik@rawtxt.in.

We will respond to all data rights requests within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

7. Cookies and Tracking

The Service uses functional cookies required for authentication (session tokens issued by Supabase Auth). We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking cookies. The PostHog analytics integration uses session recording with input masking enabled, so the content of your journal entries is never captured in recordings.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will delete the account promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify registered users by email where reasonably practicable. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or this Policy, please contact us at karthik@rawtxt.in. We aim to respond within 3 business days.
