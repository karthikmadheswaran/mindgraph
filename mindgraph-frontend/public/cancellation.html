Cancellation Policy

You are in full control of your rawtxt subscription. This page explains how cancellation works and what happens to your access and data when you cancel.

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. We do not charge cancellation fees and do not offer pro-rata refunds for the unused portion of a billing period.

How to Cancel

Cancellation is self-service. To cancel your subscription:

  • Log in to your rawtxt account at rawtxt.in.
  • Go to account settings.
  • Select "Cancel Subscription" and confirm.

Your subscription will be marked for cancellation immediately. You will receive a confirmation to the email address on your account. No further charges will be made after the current billing period ends.

If you are unable to cancel through account settings, contact us at karthik@rawtxt.in and we will process the cancellation manually within 1 business day.

Access After Cancellation

After cancelling, your access to all paid features continues until the last day of your current billing period. For example:

  • If you are on a monthly plan billed on the 10th of each month and you cancel on the 20th, your access continues through the 9th of the following month.
  • If you are on an annual plan and you cancel partway through the year, your access continues through the end of the annual period you paid for.

Once the billing period ends, your account reverts to a free tier. Your data (journal entries, entities, and other records) is retained and remains accessible. Nothing is deleted when you cancel.

Pro-rata Refunds

We do not offer pro-rata refunds for unused time remaining in a billing period at the point of cancellation. If you cancel a monthly subscription mid-month, you will not receive a partial refund for the remaining days.

If you cancel within 7 days of your original purchase, you are eligible for a full refund under our Refund Policy. That policy applies to first-time purchases and is separate from this cancellation policy.

Reactivating Your Subscription

You can resubscribe at any time from your account settings. All your data will still be there. There is no re-signup fee and no waiting period.

Account Deletion

Cancelling your subscription does not delete your account or your data. If you would like your account and all associated data permanently deleted, please contact us at karthik@rawtxt.in with the subject "Account Deletion Request." We will process the deletion within 30 days. See our Privacy Policy for more detail on data deletion.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cancellation Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. The policy in effect at the time of cancellation applies.

Contact

For cancellation assistance or questions about this policy, contact us at karthik@rawtxt.in.
