Refund Policy

We want you to be satisfied with rawtxt. If the Service does not meet your expectations, this policy describes how to request a refund.

We offer a full refund on any paid subscription within 7 days of the purchase date. No lengthy explanations required. Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days back to your original payment method.

Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund:

  • Your refund request must be submitted within 7 days of the original purchase date.
  • The subscription must not have been previously refunded under this policy.
  • The request must come from the email address associated with the account.

Both monthly and annual subscriptions are eligible for a refund within this window. Renewal charges (not first-time purchases) are not eligible for refund after the 7-day window has passed, as access has already been provided for the renewed period.

How to Request a Refund

Send an email to karthik@rawtxt.in with the subject line "Refund Request." Please include the email address on your account and the approximate date of purchase. We will acknowledge your request within 1 business day and process the refund within 5 to 7 business days.

You do not need to provide a reason for the refund, though feedback is welcome and helps us improve the product.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method (credit card, debit card, or UPI account) used at the time of purchase. Processing times depend on your bank or payment provider and are typically 5 to 7 business days after we initiate the refund. We have no control over how quickly your bank applies the credit.

Once a refund is processed, your subscription will be cancelled and your access to paid features will end immediately.

Free Trial

rawtxt offers a 14-day free trial with no payment information required. You will only be charged if you explicitly choose to subscribe after the trial period. If you do not subscribe, your account simply remains on the free tier at no cost. No refund request is needed for trial accounts.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. The policy in effect at the time of your purchase applies to your refund eligibility.

Contact

For refund requests or questions about this policy, contact us at karthik@rawtxt.in.
